Story

Greenvalley was founded in 2004 at Oregon, was first a university project made by two developers in order to preserve the enviroment around the university, some years later they got a huge investment which launch the project to a new level where it could be able to apply around the world.

Every idea is valid

There are some methods nowadays that may look irrelevant after so much digging but are pretty useful, like Recycling.
This action help a lot of people and industries to get new raw material to build new eletronics, bottles, clothes and plenty other things.
